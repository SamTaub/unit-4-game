Crystal Collector

Game Rules

Stats

At the start of the game a Magic Number will appear in the "Stats" window.
Each Crystal holds a secret value that will be added to your score when you click the crystal. The crystals value is hidden until you click on it for the first time.
Your goal is to match your score with the Magic Number. If you go over the Magic Number, you lose.
The Magic Number and Secret values will change upon each new game. Good Luck!
